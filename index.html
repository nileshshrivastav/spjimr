SPJIMR: a different kind of business school

In an era of continuos disruption, the world of business is changing fast. Today,s world is dynamic. but imperfect and leaders and organisations need to innovate and scale wisely to build a better world.

SPIJMR is the destination for leaders who are willing to embrace disruption and want to make a meaningful differnce in the world.

The school,s contemporary curriculam and innovative pedagogy are enriched by a practice focused view and seek to shape individuals into global industry leaders ready to embrace new ways of thinking.

Why SPJIMR?

As an innovative and socially consious management institution. SPIJMR is committed to fostering respnsible innovation that benifits society. The school is known across the student community for its teaching excellence and across industry for the quality of its graduates.
